About Us

Company Overview

Tracer Energy is a locally owned and operated Oil and Gas service company in Peace River, Alberta. We have a variety of services that include chemical hauling and transfer, pressure truck services, chemical storage, and methanol for pressure testing.

All our trucks are fully certified and our operators have several years of experience handling any type of chemical.
